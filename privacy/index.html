Privacy Policy

Solomon Nights. Last updated 16 June 2026.

This policy explains what happens to information when you visit the Solomon Nights website at solomonnights.com and music.solomonnights.com.

What we collect

We do not ask you for personal details and we do not run accounts on this site. When you visit, standard analytics and advertising tools collect limited technical information such as your device type, general location, pages viewed, and whether you clicked through to Spotify. This helps us understand which songs people enjoy and how our adverts perform.

Advertising and measurement

We use the Meta Pixel from Meta Platforms to measure and improve our adverts on Facebook and Instagram. It records anonymous events such as a page view or a click on a Listen button. We do not sell your information. You can control ad personalisation in your Facebook and Instagram settings, and through your browser and device privacy controls.

Links to other services

Our pages link out to Spotify and other streaming and social platforms. Once you follow one of those links, the privacy policy of that service applies. We have no control over how those services handle your information.

Cookies

The measurement tools above may set cookies or similar identifiers. You can block or delete cookies in your browser at any time. The site still works without them.

Contact

For any privacy question, email hello@solomonnights.com and we will respond.
